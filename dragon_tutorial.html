European Dragon Art Tutorial

Step 1

In order to establish the dragon's pose, draw a circle for a head and a line for the spine.

Step 2

Next, add a parallel line below the spine which give the impression of a 3D body. Connect it to the head and tail, and add circles where the legs will start.

Step 3

Draw rectanges with round bottoms to define the legs.

Step 4

Create the snout with a long trapezoid and add eyes by beginning ovals on the trapezoid's corners.

Step 5

Add horns or ears onto the back of the head if needed. Add a circle to signify the wing joint.

Step 6

Draw a curve starting at the circle and follow it with a long line. Mirror this behind the body for the other wing.

Step 7

Add a parallel line above this and connect the two with tight coiled lines to signify furled wings.

Step 8

Add spines, scales, tail fins, claws, and any other extra details.

Step 9

Clean up your sketch!
