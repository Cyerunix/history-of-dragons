Salamanders

Origin

The origin of the Salamander can be found to be based off of real life salamanders, albeit the realistic counterpart to be a lot less intimidating than the mythological one. The salamanders of myth can be seen to have a lizard-like form with an ability to control fire.

Folklore

Known to create and put of fires, the salamanders were very resiliant to hot temperatures as it was one of their signature abilities. Sometimes, salamanders could take on fairy-like humanoid statures. Others would be used to keep furnances burning day-in and day-out.
