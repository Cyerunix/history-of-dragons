Heraldry

Oftentimes represented on English war banners during the early 8th century, Wyverns, overtime, became a symbol of courage and strength. Now, wyverns can be seen as a common logo or mascot, seeing lots of representation, especially in the country of Wales.

Anatomy

Wyverns are similar in body structure to dragons, but instead of having four legs and wings, their front legs are merged into their wings, leaving them with just four limbs instead of six.
